Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a visit to the petting zoo?

You can book a visit by calling us at +971 3 799 2000, emailing info@alainzoo.ae, or using our online booking form. We recommend booking at least 24 hours in advance, especially for weekends and holidays.

What are the admission prices?

Admission prices vary by age and package. Adults: AED 50, Children (3-12): AED 30, Family packages available. Special rates for groups of 10 or more. Contact us for detailed pricing information.

Is the petting zoo suitable for young children?

Yes! Our petting zoo is designed to be safe and enjoyable for children of all ages. We have specially trained staff to supervise interactions and ensure everyone's safety.

Do you offer educational programs for schools?

Yes, we offer comprehensive educational programs for schools and groups. Our programs include guided tours, hands-on activities, and educational materials. Contact us to discuss your group's specific needs.

What should I bring for my visit?

We recommend bringing comfortable closed-toe shoes, sunscreen, a hat, and a camera. We provide hand sanitizer and have facilities for hand washing throughout the zoo.

Is parking available?

Yes, we have ample free parking available for all visitors. The parking area is located near the main entrance for easy access.
