Your Zoo Visit Journey

1. Entry & Welcome

Arrive at the zoo, collect your tickets, and get a warm welcome from our staff. Receive your visitor map and feeding schedule.

2. Animal Feeding

Feed friendly giraffes, goats, and sheep with special treats. Learn about their diet and feeding habits from our experts.

3. Guided Tour

Join our educational tour to learn about animal behavior, conservation efforts, and the importance of wildlife protection.

4. Photo Sessions

Capture amazing memories with our animals. Professional photographers available for special moments and family portraits.

5. Refreshments

Enjoy a break at our café with animal-themed snacks and drinks. Perfect time to relax and share your experiences.

6. Souvenir Shop

Visit our gift shop for animal-themed souvenirs, educational books, and toys to remember your amazing zoo adventure.
