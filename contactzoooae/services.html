Frequently Asked Questions

What should I wear to the petting zoo?

We recommend comfortable, closed-toe shoes and clothes you don't mind getting a little dirty. Avoid loose jewelry and bring a hat for sun protection.

Are the animals safe to interact with?

Yes, all our animals are well-trained and accustomed to human interaction. Our staff supervises all encounters to ensure safety for both visitors and animals.

Can I bring my own food for the animals?

For the safety of our animals, we only allow our specially prepared food. This ensures proper nutrition and prevents any health issues.

Do you offer group discounts?

Yes! We offer special rates for groups of 10 or more. Contact us for custom pricing and to arrange your group visit.

What age groups are your programs suitable for?

Our programs are designed for all ages, from toddlers to adults. We have age-appropriate activities and educational content for every visitor.

How far in advance should I book?

We recommend booking at least 24 hours in advance, especially for weekends and holidays. Popular programs may require booking weeks ahead.
