Delivery Information

Delivery Areas & Times ▼

We deliver throughout Gatineau and surrounding areas. Our delivery zones include:

  • Gatineau: 30-45 minutes
  • Hull: 35-50 minutes
  • Ottawa (downtown): 45-60 minutes
  • Aylmer: 40-55 minutes

Minimum Order: $15.00

Delivery Fee: $3.99 (free on orders over $30)

Pickup Options ▼

Prefer to pick up your order? We offer convenient pickup options:

  • In-Store Pickup: Available at our location - 1100 Boulevard Maloney O
  • Curbside Pickup: Call when you arrive and we'll bring your order to your car
  • Pickup Time: Usually ready in 20-30 minutes

No delivery fee for pickup orders!

Payment Methods ▼

We accept various payment methods for your convenience:

  • Cash
  • Credit Cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express)
  • Debit Cards
  • Online Payment (via our website)
  • Mobile Payment (Apple Pay, Google Pay)
