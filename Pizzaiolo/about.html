About Pizzaiolo

Learn more about our passion for authentic Italian pizza

Our Story

Pizzaiolo was born from a passion for authentic Italian cuisine and a commitment to bringing the finest pizza experience to homes across Canada. Our journey began with a simple mission: to create pizzas that honor traditional Italian recipes while embracing the highest quality ingredients.

Located in the heart of Gatineau, Quebec, we take pride in crafting each pizza with care and attention to detail. Our recipes have been refined over years, combining time-honored techniques with fresh, locally sourced ingredients whenever possible.

Our Values

Quality First

We never compromise on the quality of our ingredients. Every pizza is made with the freshest produce and finest cheeses.

Authentic Recipes

Our recipes are inspired by traditional Italian methods, ensuring an authentic taste in every bite.

Customer Satisfaction

Your satisfaction is our priority. We strive to deliver not just pizza, but an exceptional dining experience.

Frequently Asked Questions

What makes your pizza authentic Italian? ▼

We use traditional Italian recipes passed down through generations, authentic Italian cheeses, and our dough is made fresh daily using time-honored techniques. Our pizzaiolos are trained in traditional Italian pizza-making methods.

Do you offer gluten-free options? ▼

Yes! We offer gluten-free crust options for all our pizzas. Please mention this when placing your order, and we'll prepare your pizza with our certified gluten-free crust.

How long does delivery take? ▼

Our standard delivery time is 30-45 minutes, depending on your location and order volume. During peak hours, delivery may take up to 60 minutes. We always aim to deliver your pizza hot and fresh!

Can I customize my pizza? ▼

Absolutely! You can add or remove any toppings, choose your preferred crust, and even create your own custom pizza. Just let us know your preferences when ordering.

Do you cater for events? ▼

Yes, we offer catering services for events, parties, and corporate functions. Please contact us at least 48 hours in advance to discuss your catering needs and we'll create a custom menu for your event.
