Learn More About Our Pizzas

Classic Pizzas - Traditional Italian Favorites ▼

Our classic pizzas feature time-honored Italian recipes that have been perfected over generations. These pizzas represent the essence of authentic Italian cuisine, with simple yet flavorful combinations of the finest ingredients.

Featured Classics:

  • Margherita: The quintessential Italian pizza with fresh tomatoes, mozzarella di bufala, and basil - a true classic
  • Pepperoni: A beloved favorite with premium pepperoni and mozzarella cheese
Specialty Pizzas - Unique Flavor Combinations ▼

Our specialty pizzas showcase creative combinations of premium ingredients, offering unique flavors that set us apart. Each specialty pizza is carefully crafted to deliver an exceptional dining experience.

Popular Specialties:

  • Hawaiian: A tropical delight with ham, pineapple, and mozzarella
  • Meat Lovers: For carnivores - pepperoni, sausage, ham, and bacon
  • Supreme: The ultimate combination of meats and vegetables
  • BBQ Chicken: Grilled chicken with tangy BBQ sauce and red onions
  • Buffalo Chicken: Spicy buffalo chicken with blue cheese
  • Canadian: A Canadian favorite with bacon, pepperoni, and mushrooms
Vegetarian Options - Fresh and Flavorful ▼

Our vegetarian pizzas prove that you don't need meat to create incredible flavor. We use the freshest vegetables and premium cheeses to create satisfying vegetarian options.

Vegetarian Selection:

  • Veggie Delight: Bell peppers, mushrooms, onions, olives, and tomatoes
  • Four Cheese: A cheese lover's dream with mozzarella, cheddar, parmesan, and gorgonzola
  • Mushroom: Fresh mushrooms with garlic and mozzarella
  • Pesto Delight: Basil pesto with cherry tomatoes and mozzarella
Our Ingredients - Quality You Can Taste ▼

We source only the finest ingredients to ensure every pizza meets our high standards:

  • Dough: Made fresh daily using Italian 00 flour and natural yeast
  • Cheese: Premium mozzarella and authentic Italian cheeses
  • Tomatoes: San Marzano tomatoes for our sauce
  • Meats: Premium quality, no fillers or preservatives
  • Vegetables: Fresh, locally sourced when possible
