Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book tickets?

▼

You can book tickets directly through our website by clicking "Book Tickets" on any attraction page, or contact us via email or phone. We also offer group bookings for 10+ people with special discounts.

What is your cancellation policy?

▼

Cancellation policies vary by attraction. Most tickets can be rescheduled up to 48 hours before the visit date. Refunds are processed within 5-10 business days. Please check specific terms when booking.

Do you offer group discounts?

▼

Yes! We offer special rates for groups of 10 or more people. Contact us at tickets@dubaifamilyadventure.ae for group booking arrangements and customized packages.

How far in advance should I book?

▼

We recommend booking at least 3-5 days in advance, especially during peak season (October-April) and weekends. Last-minute bookings are subject to availability.

What payment methods do you accept?

▼

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express), debit cards, and bank transfers. Payment is required in full at the time of booking.
