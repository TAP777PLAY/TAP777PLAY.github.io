10 Must-Visit Family Attractions in Dubai

December 2024

Discover the top 10 family-friendly attractions that every visitor to Dubai should experience. From theme parks to nature reserves, we've curated the best options for families looking to create unforgettable memories.

1. IMG Worlds of Adventure

The world's largest indoor theme park offers thrilling rides, character meet-and-greets, and immersive themed zones. Perfect for all ages with attractions ranging from gentle rides for toddlers to adrenaline-pumping roller coasters for teens.

2. Dubai Aquarium & Underwater Zoo

Located in Dubai Mall, this massive aquarium houses thousands of aquatic animals. Walk through the tunnel for an up-close view of sharks, rays, and colorful fish. The underwater zoo features penguins, crocodiles, and interactive exhibits.

3. Legoland Dubai

A paradise for families, featuring over 40 rides, shows, and attractions. Build and race LEGO cars, explore Miniland with iconic Dubai landmarks, and enjoy water play areas. The adjacent Legoland Water Park offers additional fun.

4. Motiongate Dubai

Experience Hollywood magic with themed zones based on DreamWorks Animation, Columbia Pictures, and Lionsgate films. From Shrek's Far Far Away to The Hunger Games, there's something for every movie fan in the family.

5. KidZania Dubai

An educational theme park where family members role-play real-world professions. Everyone can become pilots, doctors, chefs, and more while learning valuable life skills. Perfect for interactive family experiences.

6. OliOli Museum

An interactive museum designed to spark curiosity and creativity. With 8 galleries featuring hands-on exhibits, family members can explore science, art, and technology through play. Ideal for interactive learning experiences.

7. Dubai Parks and Resorts

A massive entertainment destination combining multiple theme parks, hotels, and dining options. Includes Legoland, Motiongate, Bollywood Parks, and Riverland Dubai. One ticket gives access to multiple experiences.

8. Aquaventure Waterpark

Located at Atlantis The Palm, this award-winning waterpark features record-breaking slides, a lazy river, and a private beach. The Splashers area is perfect for families with gentle slides and pools.

9. Dubai Miracle Garden

The world's largest natural flower garden features over 50 million flowers arranged in stunning displays. Families will love the themed areas, butterfly garden, and colorful photo opportunities. Open seasonally from November to May.

10. Dubai Desert Conservation Reserve

Experience authentic Arabian culture with desert safaris, camel rides, and traditional entertainment. Many tours are family-friendly and include sandboarding, falconry shows, and Bedouin camp experiences with dinner under the stars.

Planning Tips

  • Purchase tickets online in advance for better prices
  • Check height requirements before visiting theme parks
  • Many attractions offer family packages and discounts
  • Consider the Dubai Parks and Resorts multi-park pass for maximum value
  • Book desert safaris during cooler months (November to March)
