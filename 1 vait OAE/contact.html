Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our safari breakfast experiences

We recommend wearing comfortable, loose-fitting clothing in light colors. Long pants and long sleeves are ideal for protection from the sun and sand. Closed-toe shoes are essential, and don't forget a hat and sunglasses. We provide traditional Arabic headscarves (keffiyeh) for an authentic experience.

Our experiences typically start between 5:30 AM and 6:30 AM, depending on the season and sunrise times. We'll provide you with the exact pickup time when you book. The early start ensures you witness the beautiful desert sunrise and enjoy the cooler morning temperatures.

Yes, all our experiences include round-trip transportation from your hotel or a designated pickup point in Dubai, Abu Dhabi, or Sharjah. We use comfortable, air-conditioned vehicles suitable for desert terrain. Pickup times and locations will be confirmed upon booking.

In case of severe weather conditions (sandstorms, heavy rain, or extreme temperatures), we may need to reschedule your experience for safety reasons. We'll contact you as soon as possible to arrange an alternative date or provide a full refund. Light winds and normal desert conditions don't affect our operations.

Yes, our experiences are family-friendly and suitable for children aged 5 and above. We provide special safety equipment for children and can adjust activities to be more suitable for younger participants. Please inform us of any children in your group when booking so we can make appropriate arrangements.

Our breakfast includes traditional Emirati dishes like balaleet, machboos, and luqaimat, as well as international options. Fresh fruits, Arabic coffee, tea, and water are also provided. We can accommodate dietary restrictions and preferences - please inform us when booking.

Yes, you can cancel or reschedule your booking up to 24 hours before your scheduled experience for a full refund. Cancellations within 24 hours are subject to a 50% cancellation fee. We understand that plans can change, so we try to be as flexible as possible with rescheduling.

Yes, we offer both private experiences for couples or small groups, and larger group experiences for families or corporate events. Private experiences can be customized to your preferences, while group experiences follow our standard itinerary. Contact us to discuss your specific needs.
