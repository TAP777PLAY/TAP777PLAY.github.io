Our Story

2016

The Beginning

Founded by a group of passionate Emirati locals and international tourism experts, Safari Breakfasts UAE started with a simple mission: to share the authentic beauty of the Arabian desert with visitors from around the world.

2018

Expansion & Recognition

We expanded our operations to cover multiple desert locations across the UAE and received recognition from the Dubai Tourism Board for our commitment to sustainable tourism practices.

2021

Digital Innovation

Launched our innovative route builder and online booking system, making it easier for guests to plan and customize their desert breakfast experiences.

2025

Today

We continue to be the leading provider of authentic safari breakfast experiences in the UAE, serving thousands of guests annually while maintaining our commitment to cultural preservation and environmental sustainability.

Our Mission

To provide authentic, safe, and memorable desert breakfast experiences that celebrate Emirati culture while promoting sustainable tourism and environmental conservation in the UAE's most beautiful desert locations.

Our Values

Authenticity

We preserve and share genuine Emirati traditions, ensuring every experience reflects the true spirit of the Arabian desert culture.

Safety First

The safety and comfort of our guests is our top priority. We maintain the highest safety standards and use modern equipment.

Sustainability

We are committed to environmental conservation and sustainable tourism practices that protect the desert ecosystem.

Excellence

We strive for excellence in every aspect of our service, from planning to execution, ensuring unforgettable experiences.

Community

We support local communities and employ local guides who share their knowledge and passion for the desert.

Innovation

We continuously innovate our services and use technology to enhance guest experiences while maintaining cultural authenticity.
