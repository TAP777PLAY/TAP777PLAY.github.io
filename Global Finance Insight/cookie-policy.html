1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your computer, smartphone, or other device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently and to provide information to website owners.

Cookies allow a website to recognize a user's device and remember information about their visit, such as their preferred language and other settings. This can make your next visit easier and the site more useful to you.

Quick Facts About Cookies

• Cookies cannot harm your computer or device
• They don't contain viruses or malware
• They typically contain anonymous information
• You can control and delete cookies through your browser settings
