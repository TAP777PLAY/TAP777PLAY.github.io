Cookies Policy

Last updated: 20 October 2025

1. What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device to help the site function, improve performance, and remember your preferences.

2. How we use cookies

  • Essential cookies: required for core functionality (e.g., security, forms).
  • Preferences: remember choices such as theme and language.
  • Analytics (optional): measure usage to improve content.

3. Managing cookies

You can accept or decline cookies via our on‑site banner. You can also manage cookies in your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect site functionality.

4. Third‑party services

Embedded content (e.g., Google Maps, videos) may set their own cookies. Please review those providers’ policies.
