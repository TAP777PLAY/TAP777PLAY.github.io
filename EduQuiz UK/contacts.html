Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly do you respond to messages?

+

We typically respond to all inquiries within 24 hours during business days. For urgent technical support, we aim to respond within 4 hours.

Can I visit your office in person?

+

Yes! We welcome visitors to our London office. Please contact us in advance to schedule a meeting, as we want to ensure someone is available to assist you.

Do you offer educational partnerships?

+

Absolutely! We're always interested in partnering with educational institutions, organizations, and content creators. Please reach out to discuss potential collaborations.

What if I have technical issues with the platform?

+

For technical support, please email us with a detailed description of the issue, including your browser type and any error messages. We'll work to resolve it quickly.
