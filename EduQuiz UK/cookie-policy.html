Cookie Policy

This Cookie Policy explains how EduQuiz UK uses cookies and similar technologies when you visit our website.

Last updated: January 2025

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your computer or mobile device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently and to provide information to website owners.

How We Use Cookies

EduQuiz UK uses cookies to:

  • Remember your preferences and settings
  • Track your quiz progress and learning journey
  • Improve website performance and user experience
  • Analyze how our website is used to help us improve our services
  • Provide personalized content and recommendations

Types of Cookies We Use

Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for the website to function properly. They enable basic functions like page navigation, access to secure areas, and remembering your quiz progress. The website cannot function properly without these cookies.

Performance Cookies

These cookies collect information about how visitors use our website, such as which pages are visited most often. This helps us improve how our website works and ensures users find what they are looking for easily.

Functionality Cookies

These cookies allow the website to remember choices you make (such as your username, language preference, or quiz difficulty level) and provide enhanced, more personal features.

Analytics Cookies

We use analytics cookies to understand how our website is being used. This helps us improve the user experience and develop new features that our users want.

Third-Party Cookies

We may also use third-party services that set cookies on our behalf, including:

  • Google Analytics: To analyze website traffic and user behavior
  • Google Fonts: To provide web fonts for better typography
  • Social Media Platforms: For social sharing functionality

Managing Your Cookie Preferences

You can control and manage cookies in various ways:

  • Browser Settings: Most browsers allow you to refuse cookies or delete them. You can usually find these settings in the 'Options' or 'Preferences' menu of your browser.
  • Cookie Consent: When you first visit our website, you'll see a cookie consent banner where you can choose which cookies to accept.
  • Opt-out Links: For analytics cookies, you can opt out using the links provided by the respective service providers.

Impact of Disabling Cookies

If you choose to disable cookies, some features of our website may not function properly. This may include:

  • Loss of quiz progress tracking
  • Inability to save your preferences
  • Reduced personalization of content
  • Potential issues with website functionality

Updates to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the new Cookie Policy on this page.
