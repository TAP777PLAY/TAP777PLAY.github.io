Frequently Asked Questions

What activities are suitable for theme parks?

▼

Most theme parks in Dubai offer a variety of attractions suitable for all family members. However, specific rides have height requirements. Parks like Legoland and OliOli are perfect for interactive experiences, while IMG Worlds and Motiongate are better for thrill seekers. Always check height restrictions before visiting.

Are there activities for families seeking gentle experiences?

▼

Yes! Many attractions have dedicated areas with soft play, sensory activities, and quiet spaces. OliOli Museum has special interactive areas. Most water parks have splash pools suitable for all comfort levels with proper supervision. Always check facility availability before booking.

What safety measures are in place?

▼

All attractions follow strict UAE safety regulations. Lifeguards are present at water parks, height restrictions are enforced at theme parks, and first aid stations are available throughout. Staff are trained in safety and emergency procedures. We recommend keeping family members together and following all safety guidelines.
