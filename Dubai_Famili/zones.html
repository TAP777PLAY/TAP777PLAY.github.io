Detailed Information

IMG Worlds of Adventure - Complete Guide

▼

Location: Sheikh Mohammed Bin Zayed Road, Dubai

Opening Hours: 11:00 AM - 10:00 PM (varies by season)

Highlights: The world's largest indoor theme park featuring Marvel Super Heroes, Cartoon Network, Lost Valley Dinosaur Adventure, and IMG Boulevard zones. Over 20 rides and attractions including roller coasters, dark rides, and interactive experiences.

Best Time to Visit: Weekday mornings for shorter queues. Avoid weekends and public holidays.

Tips: Download the IMG Worlds app for ride wait times. Consider the Fast Track pass during peak season.

Motiongate Dubai - Hollywood Experience

▼

Location: Dubai Parks and Resorts, Jebel Ali

Opening Hours: 10:00 AM - 8:00 PM

Highlights: Three themed zones: DreamWorks (Shrek, Kung Fu Panda, How to Train Your Dragon), Sony Pictures (The Green Hornet, Ghostbusters), and Lionsgate (The Hunger Games). 27 rides and attractions including the world's first John Wick-themed ride.

Best Time to Visit: Early morning or late afternoon. The park is less crowded during weekdays.

Tips: Purchase combo tickets with Legoland and Bollywood Parks for better value. Check show schedules in advance.

Legoland Dubai - Building Fun

▼

Location: Dubai Parks and Resorts, Jebel Ali

Opening Hours: 10:00 AM - 6:00 PM

Highlights: 40+ interactive rides, shows, and attractions. Miniland featuring Dubai landmarks built from LEGO bricks. Water park and hotel available on-site.

Best Time to Visit: Weekdays are ideal. The park is designed for interactive family experiences, perfect for families seeking creative activities.

Tips: Combine with Legoland Water Park for a full day experience. The park is stroller-friendly with plenty of rest areas.

KidZania Dubai - Educational Fun

▼

Location: The Dubai Mall, Downtown Dubai

Opening Hours: 10:00 AM - 10:00 PM

Highlights: A family-sized city where family members can role-play over 80 different professions. Earn KidZos (currency) by completing activities. Realistic settings including hospital, fire station, TV studio, and more.

Best Time to Visit: Morning sessions are less crowded. Allow 3-4 hours for a complete experience.

Tips: Book online in advance. Family members receive a bank card to track their earnings. Everyone can participate in the interactive experiences.
