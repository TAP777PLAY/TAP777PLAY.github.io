Cookie Policy

Last Updated: December 2025

This Cookie Policy explains how Dubai Family Adventure ("we," "us," or "our") uses cookies and similar tracking technologies on our website and mobile application (collectively, the "Service").

By using our Service, you consent to the use of cookies in accordance with this policy. This Cookie Policy should be read together with our Privacy Policy.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device (computer, tablet, or mobile phone) when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently and provide information to website owners.

Cookies allow a website to recognize your device and store some information about your preferences or past actions. We also use similar technologies such as web beacons, pixel tags, and local storage.

2. Types of Cookies We Use

Cookie Type Purpose Duration Third Party
Essential Cookies Required for basic functionality (authentication, security, preferences) Session / 1 year No
Analytics Cookies Help us understand how visitors use our Service (Google Analytics) 2 years Yes (Google)
Functional Cookies Remember your preferences and settings (language, region) 1 year No
Advertising Cookies Deliver relevant advertisements and measure campaign effectiveness 90 days Yes (Google Ads)
Performance Cookies Monitor website performance and identify issues 30 days No

3. Specific Cookies We Use

Essential Cookies

▼

These cookies are necessary for the Service to function and cannot be switched off:

  • session_id: Maintains your session while browsing
  • csrf_token: Security token to prevent cross-site request forgery
  • cookie_consent: Remembers your cookie preferences
  • user_preferences: Stores your language and region settings

Google Analytics Cookies

▼

We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors interact with our Service:

  • _ga: Distinguishes unique users (2 years)
  • _gid: Distinguishes unique users (24 hours)
  • _gat: Throttles request rate (1 minute)
  • _ga_*: Persists session state (2 years)

You can opt-out of Google Analytics by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

Advertising Cookies

▼

We use Google Ads and other advertising platforms to show relevant ads:

  • _gcl_*: Google conversion tracking (90 days)
  • IDE: Google DoubleClick advertising (2 years)
  • test_cookie: Checks if browser supports cookies (session)

You can manage your advertising preferences through Google Ads Settings.

4. Local Storage and Similar Technologies

In addition to cookies, we use other technologies to store information on your device:

  • Local Storage: Stores your route planning preferences and saved itineraries
  • Session Storage: Temporarily stores data during your browsing session
  • Web Beacons: Small images that help us track email opens and page views
  • Pixel Tags: Used to measure the effectiveness of our marketing campaigns

These technologies work similarly to cookies and are subject to the same privacy protections.

You can manage these permissions through your device settings or within the app.

8. Do Not Track Signals

Some browsers include a "Do Not Track" (DNT) feature that signals websites you visit that you do not want to have your online activity tracked. Currently, there is no standard for how DNT signals should be interpreted.

We do not currently respond to DNT browser signals. However, you can control tracking through our cookie consent banner and browser settings.
